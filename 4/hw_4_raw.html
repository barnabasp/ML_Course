  • interpret the coefficients and their meaning shortly with your own words
  • plot the residuals for the predictions. if you had to decide only on this information, which house would you buy?

The Y functions looks like the following: Y price = $5.78 \cdot X_1$ transaction date $+ (-0.25) \cdot X_2$ house age $+ (-0.01)\cdot X_3$ distance to MRT (mass rapid transit) $+ 1.26 \cdot X_4$ # convenience stores

Based on the coefficients: We can see that Y is increased by the number of convenience stores slightly and more with transaction date. House age is slightly decreasing the price, distance to mass rapid transit doesn't count really.
